# AUVORA MOMENTUM &bull; coderadi

> v0.9.0 | Pre-release

## BRIEF
Auvora Momentum is a minimal web app that allows users to track their habits, streaks, and build a momentum. That's why it's called Auvora **Momentum**.

## FLOW
```py
open app > create account/login > done.
open app > dashboard > mark habits > done.
open calender > review monthly score > adjust.
open profile > add new habit > done.
```

## NAVIGATION
This app will have three pages to track the habits and your future self.
1. **Dashboard**: A summary of all.
2. **Calender**: Monthly performance, concluded.
3. **Profile**: The visualization of your future self, habits list.
